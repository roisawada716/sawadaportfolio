About Me

I started playing the guitar back in late 2019. I am a guitar player learning mostly from different YouTube videos. I give my best to do my job as a lead guitar player, as well as to follow the dynamics of the sound of the band I am with. My first instrument was the drumkit. I developed a foundation in muscle memory from my time behind the kit. Years later, I was outgrew the drums and looked for another instrument, and the instrument was the guitar.
